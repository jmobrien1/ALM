first and, by doing so, create a stronger, safer more prosperous

security America will remain united through the commonality of
citizenship -- regardless of race, religion. national orientation,
gender or sexual orientation.   As proud Americans, Build the Wall
Donors respect the fact they are citizens of the greatest country in
the World and feel those who live here illegally threaten our
country’s social cohesion and democracy.
